Holistic recruiting that re-connects hiring managers and candidates directly — stripping out the admin drag, and giving every candidate honest feedback on where they fell short and how to grow.

1 Getting in

One door, three experts. No "candidate vs. recruiter" silos — everyone picks the hat they're wearing today.

✎ no separate logins or silos — the same person is often a candidate and an expert reviewer. One identity.

2 The Candidate

Build a holistic profile, get matched, and — win or lose — walk away knowing exactly where you stand.

✎ the "twist": fit is shown as hard vs soft sub-scores — and candidates see their own ranking. No black box.
✎ EVERY rejection auto-generates this. Soft vs hard, measured against the role's bar — then a concrete path to fix it. This is the USP made visible.

3 The Hiring Manager

Sees real humans, not stacks of CVs filtered by someone who never met the team.

✎ the admin (scheduling, screening, feedback drafting) is automated — so the manager spends time on the human bit: the conversation.

4 The Freelance Recruiter

Optional facilitator, paid for outcomes — not for forwarding emails.

✎ recruiters become optional matchmakers / coaches, not gatekeepers. They compete on judgment, not on hoarding CVs.

5 Future — Upskilling Marketplace roadmap

Feedback closes a loop: rejected for a skill gap? Holicruit routes you to a course or program that fixes it.

✎ this is the flywheel: rejection → honest feedback → targeted learning → re-match. Education partners are the future revenue line. Finishing a course updates the profile & re-runs matches automatically.
