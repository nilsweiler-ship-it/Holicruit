Hi {First name},

Hiring today runs on two broken defaults: a firehose of cold applications you can't fairly read, and silent rejections that quietly damage your employer brand. Holicruit removes both.

We're a holistic, opt-in recruiting platform. Instead of keyword-matching résumés, we measure the whole person — hard skills, scenario-assessed soft skills (never self-rated), and verified evidence — against the bar *you* define for the role. You only ever see candidates where there's genuine two-sided fit, each one already scored on hard fit, soft fit, and mutual fit.

Two things hiring teams tell us they care about most:

- **A pipeline, not a pile.** Drag candidates through stages, see each one's fit breakdown and verified skills, and chat directly with inline scheduling. No inbox archaeology.
- **Rejection that builds your brand.** When you pass, we auto-draft an honest Growth Report for the candidate — where they stood and how to close the gap. Candidates leave respecting you, not resenting you. Recruiters on the platform are paid on outcomes only, so incentives stay aligned.
