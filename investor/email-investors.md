**Subject:** The honest recruiting platform — seed round

Hi {First name},

I'm building Holicruit because hiring is broken on both sides and everyone knows it.

**Candidates** are ghosted — 75% never hear back, and 89% say they'd take an honest "no" over silence. **Employers** fly just as blind: a bad hire costs at least 30% of first-year salary, 46% of new hires fail within 18 months, and 89% of those failures trace to soft skills a keyword scan can't see.
